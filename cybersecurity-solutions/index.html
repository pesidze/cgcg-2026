What We Cover

Every layer of your security posture. Fully covered.

Cybersecurity at CGCG isn't a single product — it's a comprehensive program covering every layer of your attack surface, end to end.

01

Endpoint Protection

Every device. Every potential entry point. Covered.

CGCG deploys and manages next-generation endpoint detection and response (EDR) across your entire device fleet — laptops, desktops, servers, mobile — with behavioral analysis that catches threats traditional antivirus misses.

Plain English

EDR watches what every device is actually doing — not just looking for known viruses, but spotting suspicious behavior. That's how we catch attacks that have never been seen before.

02

Email Security

Over 90% of attacks begin with an email.

CGCG deploys multi-layer email security — filtering, anti-phishing, impersonation detection, and link sandboxing — so malicious emails never reach your team's inbox.

Security note

We also run phishing simulations against your team to identify who needs training — before a real attacker does.

03

Vulnerability Management

Find weaknesses before attackers do.

We continuously scan your environment for known vulnerabilities, misconfigured systems, and outdated software — and patch them before attackers can exploit them. Scheduled scans, continuous monitoring, and prioritized remediation, all managed.

04

Identity & Access Management

Stolen credentials are the #1 attack vector.

We enforce multi-factor authentication (MFA), manage privileged access, deploy single sign-on (SSO), and ensure the right people have access to the right things — and no one else does.

Plain English

If an attacker steals your password, MFA means they still can't get in. We make sure MFA is enforced across every system your team uses.

05

Network Security

Your perimeter, designed and managed to hold.

Your network perimeter — firewalls, VPNs, segmentation, intrusion detection — designed and managed to keep threats out and contain them if they get in. We monitor traffic patterns and alert on anomalies before they become incidents.

06

Cloud Security

Your cloud is not secure by default.

CGCG audits and hardens your AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud configurations, manages security policies, and monitors cloud activity for unauthorized access or data exfiltration.

07

Compliance & Risk Management

SOC 2, HIPAA, GDPR, PCI DSS, NIST — we own it.

CGCG maps your controls to each framework, maintains evidence for audits, and keeps your compliance posture current as regulations evolve. We handle the documentation; you handle the business.

08

Incident Response

When something happens — because at some point, it does.

CGCG responds: contain the threat, preserve evidence, identify the root cause, remediate the damage, and document everything your leadership, legal, and insurance teams will need.

Security note

Incident response without a pre-built plan is chaos. We build your IR plan before you need it — so when the moment comes, everyone knows exactly what to do.
