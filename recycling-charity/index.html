Why This Matters

Old hardware is a data security risk until it isn't.

A retired laptop still holds data. A decommissioned server still has cached credentials. Most businesses don't think about this until it's too late.

A retired laptop sitting in a closet still contains data. A decommissioned server still has drive images, cached credentials, and application data that a determined attacker could recover. Most businesses don't think about this until it's too late. CGCG solves it cleanly — certified data destruction, written certificate, compliance documentation.

For devices still able to serve someone, we coordinate donation to schools, nonprofits, and community organizations in the New York area. NIST 800-88 is the federal government's standard for securely erasing data from storage media. It's the standard that holds up in court, in audits, and with cyber insurers. We certify to it.

How It Works

From collection to certification — completely handled.

We manage every step so you don't have to think about it. One call, one pickup, one certificate — and the problem is solved.

STEP 01

Inventory

We catalog every device — make, model, serial number, condition — before anything moves. Chain of custody starts here.

STEP 02

Collect

We schedule a pickup at your location. No logistics burden on your team — we handle transport and handling.

STEP 03

Data Destruction

Every storage device is wiped to NIST 800-88 standards. Physical destruction available for drives that require it.

STEP 04

Sort & Route

Functional devices go to nonprofit partners. Non-functional units go to certified recyclers. Nothing goes to landfill.

STEP 05

Certify

You receive a Certificate of Destruction, asset manifest, and donation documentation — ready for auditors and insurers.

Every type of device. Responsibly retired.

From a single laptop to an entire office decommission — we handle every category of IT equipment with the same rigorous process.

01

Computers & Laptops

Desktops, laptops, all-in-ones, Macs, PCs.

All storage media wiped to NIST 800-88 standards before any further handling. Certificate of destruction issued per device.

02

Servers & Storage

Rack servers, NAS, SAN, tape libraries, drive arrays.

We coordinate full-scale decommissioning projects — from planning the pickup schedule to delivering the final asset report and certificate package.
