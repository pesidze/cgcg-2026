The Problem

Cabling installed without a plan creates problems that compound over time.

Network bottlenecks that look like software issues

Inferior cabling causes intermittent performance problems that get misdiagnosed for months — blamed on the ISP, the router, or the application — when the actual cause is in the wall.

Camera blind spots discovered after an incident

Security camera coverage looks complete on paper until a theft, an injury, or a dispute reveals a gap. Designing the cabling infrastructure first, with the coverage plan, eliminates this entirely.

AV systems that require a workaround every meeting

Conference rooms that "mostly work" are a productivity drain. Properly planned AV cabling eliminates the adapter pile, the IT ticket, and the 10-minute startup ritual before every call.

Renovation costs that triple because nothing was documented

When no one knows what's inside the walls — or the runs aren't labeled — every future upgrade becomes an exploration project. CGCG documents everything, from design to completion.
